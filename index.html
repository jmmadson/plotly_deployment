Project Overview:

Many labs have had success in the past growing meat from various types of fungi, algae, and microorganisms found on plant life.

The pupose of this study was to look for a particular set of bacteria, known to be able to synthesize protein, that is found on the human body. Below is the data set of the bacteria found in the belly buttons of the volunteer test subjects.
